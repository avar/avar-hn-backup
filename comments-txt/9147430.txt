You and mortehu upthread have mentioned Schengen in the context of being
allowed to reside in any country within the area. That's not what Schengen is
about, you're thinking of the European Economic Area.

Schengen is a customs union which allows passport-free travel within its
borders, but the EEA is the area you can freely migrate within.
